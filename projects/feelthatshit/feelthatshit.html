Browser based window shopping.

An online interaction model that gives the visitor a very similar experience to window shopping in the real world.

There is a certain kind of feeling you get when walking carefree down a street past the windows of boutiques. The visual merchandising of the window, the music that is playing inside the shop, and the overall attitude of the brand comes through the moment you see a store. Each of these elements permeates your senses and stimulates the shopper.

The experience a shopper has in a real store is so drastically different that the clinical environments we have continually refined and adapted for the web.

E-Commerce has become more an analytically driven science than a fun experience.

This project creates a seamless relationship between the experience and the product itself.

The two are inexplicably intertwined. Everything was considered from the collection to the interface to the soundscape.
