A kids book about advertising.

The ABCs of Contemporary Creatives is a book meant to inspire and educate. A kids book for adults in advertising.

The book features illustration work by Craig Ward, Tristan Eaton, I Love Dust, Gary Baseman, Julene Harrison, Rizon Parein, Vasava, Jeff Nishinaka, and more. This project was a collaboration with Bernstein & Andriulli who hooked up the artists involved. The book was released on Tumblr over the course of one day.
