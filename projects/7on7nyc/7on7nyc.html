A conference to make something new.

Presented by Rhizome, the Seven on Seven conference pairs seven leading artists with seven luminary technologists, and challenges them to make something new together. They unveil their creations, and discuss their process, at this intimate public event.

We created an adaptive visual identity generated by an SVG engine. The seven dots represent the artists, the other seven represent the technologists. They are connected randomly to create thousands of variations. These variations are used in the interactive experience and across all collateral.
